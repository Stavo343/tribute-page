A living card game from Fantasy Flight Games

Explore Middle-Earth...

The Lord of the Rings: The Card Game is a phenomonal experience set in the Third Age of Tolkien's Middle-Earth. Created by Nate French and published by Fantasy Flight Games, this living card game explores the possibilities of Tolkien's lore set in the seventeen years between Gandalf leaving the Shire and Frodo beginning his journey to Bree. The game is structured in five different forms aside from the Core Box:

  • Deluxe Expansions - These expansions are required to play any quests in their corresponding cycles. The only exception is the first cycle, Shadows of Mirkwood, where the Core Set acts as the deluxe expansion.
  • Adventure Packs - These expansions flesh out a cycle for the game. Each cycle consists of one deluxe expansion and six adventure packs, all containing a cohesive theme.
  • Saga Expansions - These deluxe expansion-sized boxes each contain three quests that follow events in The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings trilogy books. For those who want to experience the events of Tolkien's actual stories, these are excellent!
  • Print on Demand (POD) Decks - Each year for GenCon and Fellowship events, FFG releases extra special quests enriched with theme and clever mechanics for fans to gather together and enjoy playing for a thematic challenge.
  • Nightmare Packs - For the hardcore player, nightmare packs integrate themselves with their specific corresponding quests in order to give an even more challenging experience than the original quests.

Shadows of Mirkwood Cycle

Core Set - Make your way through Mirkwood battling spiders, travel down the Anduin as enemies pursue you, and escape from the dungeons of Dol Guldur in this Core Set.

  • The Hunt for Gollum - Assist Aragorn in his search for clues to track down the creature Gollum.
  • Conflict at the Carrock - Encounter a group of trolls at the Carrock and fight your way out.
  • A Journey to Rhosgobel - Escort an injured eagle to the dwelling of Radagast the Brown before it's too late.
  • The Hills of Emyn Muil - Navigate the labyrinth of rocks through Emyn Muil on your search for Gollum.
  • The Dead Marshes - Survive the treacheries lying long dead as Gollum attempts to hide and escape you.
  • Return to Mirkwood - Escort your captive Gollum back to the palace of Thranduil for safekeeping, but beware the enemies who would have him free...

Dwarrowdelf Cycle

Khazad-dûm - Enter the oily black of Moria, search through the corridors as you fend off orcs, and escape the pursuit of the Nameless Fear in this first deluxe expansion.

  • The Redhorn Gate - Brave the treacherous cold of Caradhras as you escort Lady Arwen through.
  • Road to Rivendell - Continue your journey to Lorien with Arwen, but be on guard for ambushing enemies along the way.
  • The Watcher in the Water - Fend off tentacles as you either take down the Watcher or guess the password to enter the Doors of Durin.
  • The Long Dark - Grasp your torch tightly as you search for a way out of tunnels where unseen dangers await.
  • Foundations of Stone - Shake off a nasty surprise as you become separated from your company and discover things in the deep that will not appreciate being awoken.
  • Shadow and Flame - Steel yourself for a showdown with Durin's Bane. Your life may find itself ending in a flash of flame and fury...

Against the Shadow Cycle

Heirs of Númenor - After collecting a letter in Pelargir to deliver to Denethor, assist Faramir against Southrons and Easterlings on your way and take part in defending a besieged Cair Andros.

  • The Steward's Fear - Investigate the underworld of Minas Tirith at the behest of Denethor. Dark dealings may be your undoing.
  • The Drúadan Forest - As you pursue Minas Tirith's underworld boss out of the city and into the Drúadan Forest, find yourself assailed by the Woses who you must win over before being overtaken by them.
  • Encounter at Amon Dîn - On your way back to Minas Tirith, protect a village under attack as it burns and villagers perish under the sword of a nefarious orc.
  • Assault on Osgiliath - Struggle for control of all locations in Osgiliath as you fight back against an assault.
  • The Blood of Gondor - Meet the Black Numenorean, a foe you must defeat to rescue a captured Faramir.
  • The Morgul Vale - Discover at last the underworld boss' identity and vanquish him along with a Nazgul in this climactic battle...

Ring-Maker Cycle

The Voice of Isengard - Under the influence of the White Wizard, track down and capture an orc leading you through Dunland, the Fords of Isen, and Fangorn Forest.

  • The Dunland Trap - Resist an ambush of Dunlanders whose chief in particular has a bone to pick with you.
  • The Three Trials - Prove yourself in a test to gain an alliance with the Dunlanders on Saruman's behalf.
  • Trouble in Tharbad - Assist a dwarf in escaping orcs in order to convince him to lead you to the resting place of Celebrimbor's secret.
  • The Nin-in-Eilph - Trudge through the mires of the Nin-in-Eilph as large insects and snakes wear away your endurance.
  • Celebrimbor's Secret - Search for the resting place of Celebrimbor's ring mold, that artifact that Saruman covets.
  • The Antlered Crown - Before returning to Orthanc, witness mayhem among the three Dunland tribes and fight to unite them into one people once again...

Angmar Awakened Cycle

The Lost Realm - Join the Dúnedain of the north in battling an awaking evil which kidnaps one of the rangers for some foul purpose.

  • The Wastes of Eriador - Traverse the dangerous north as you pursue the ranger captors, but beware when day turns to night.
  • Escape from Mount Gram - Sneak through a cavernous dungeon in search of your friends and equipment before escaping those harsh corridors.
  • Across the Ettenmoors - Take the only path that you can after your escape which is the home of several nasty trolls that will gladly hunt you down for a pleasant snack.
  • Treachery at Rhuduar - Investigate suspicious ruins in Rhuduar for clues to the endgame of these undead enemies assaulting the Dúnedain.
  • The Battle of Carn Dûm - Foray into the Battle of Carn Dûm in your assault against the evils in the north before their objective can be completed.
  • The Dread Realm - Stifle your fear in this climactic fight against undead sorcerors. Achieve victory before it completely awakens...

Dream-chaser Cycle

The Grey Havens - Meet a man from Dol Amroth descended from Numenor who has strange visions of trouble brewing in the now-sunken kingdom. Locate an ancient ruin and solve the mysteries therein before the competing Corsairs do.

  • Flight of the Stormcaller - Sail in your own ship to chase down the Umbar pirates fleeing out to sea.
  • The Thing in the Depths - Battle the pirates of the Stormcaller before something more menacing rises to challenge both you and your enemies.
  • Temple of the Deceived - Explore an island as you fend off undead enemies who would not let you discover their temple.
  • The Drowned Ruins - Submerge yourselves through different grottos, seeking answers under a crumbling temple and facing many perils.
  • A Storm on Cobas Haven - Race against time as you pursue a Corsair ship along the southwestern coast of Middle-Earth.
  • City of Corsairs - Witness the unfolding of the final act of the Dream-chaser cycle in the heart of enemy territory as you struggle for survival...
